Dr. Anil Kothiwala

Consultant Endovascular, Minimally Invasive Brain and Spine Surgeon


Dr. Anil Kothiwala is Consultant Endovascular, Minimally Invasive Brain and Spine Surgeon at Rajasthan Hospital, Jaipur. Before joining Rajasthan Hospital, he was working as Consultant Neurosurgeon, Department of Neurosurgery and Neurointervention at Fortis Escorts Hospital Jaipur.

He is the first RMC registered 5-year MCH program doctor of state.

He has done stroke scholar course for endovascular management of strokes from the department of Neurointervention at Medanta Hospital.

Actively involved in research and academics and have many articles in the indexed journal related to neurosurgery.
